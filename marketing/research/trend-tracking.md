# Trend Tracking Guide

## Daily Routine (15 min)

### Morning Scroll (10 min)
1. Open TikTok → For You page
2. Watch for 5-10 min, note:
   - Sounds you hear 3+ times (trending)
   - Formats/templates being repeated
   - Any sleep/alarm/morning content going viral
3. Save trending sounds to "Marketing" collection in TikTok

### Quick Check (5 min)
4. TikTok Creative Center → Trending Songs → US region
5. Search "alarm" / "wake up" / "morning" on TikTok → This week → Most liked
6. Check if any saved trends can be adapted for ScanAlarm

## Weekly Research (Sunday, 30 min)

### Trending Sound Audit
1. Review saved sounds from the week
2. For each trending sound, ask:
   - Can this be adapted to alarm/wake-up content?
   - What's the format pattern? (how are others using it?)
   - How many days old is the trend? (< 7 days = act now)
3. Script 2-3 trending audio videos for the coming week

### CapCut Template Mining
1. Open CapCut → Templates → Trending
2. Search: "morning", "routine", "alarm", "life hack"
3. Save templates that fit ScanAlarm content
4. Note the structure and adapt scripts to fit

### Hashtag Performance
1. Check which of our hashtags drove the most views
2. Identify new hashtags gaining momentum
3. Update hashtag sets in content templates

## Trending Sound Action Framework

| Sound Age | Action | Priority |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| < 3 days | Create + post within 24 hours | HIGH |
| 3-7 days | Create + post within 48 hours | MEDIUM |
| 7-14 days | Only if perfectly fits our content | LOW |
| > 14 days | Skip (trend is dying) | SKIP |

## Content Format Trends to Watch

### Evergreen Formats (always work)
- POV videos
- Before/After transformations
- "3 apps you need" lists
- Tutorial / How-to
- "Day X of..." challenge series

### Seasonal Opportunities

| Period | Angle |

| Daylight saving | "How to survive the time change" |

## Trend Adaptation Checklist

When you spot a relevant trend:

- [ ] Is the sound/format < 7 days old?
- [ ] Can it be mapped to alarm/wake-up/morning content?
- [ ] Does the format allow product showcase (screen recording or mention)?
- [ ] Can we produce it within 24 hours?
- [ ] Does it fit our brand tone (humor + relatability)?

If 4+ yes → **produce and post immediately**.
