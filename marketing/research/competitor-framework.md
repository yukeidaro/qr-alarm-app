# Competitor Research Framework

## Target Competitors

| App | Key Feature | QR Scan | Price Model | Social Presence |
|-----|------------|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Alarmy** | Photo/QR/math/shake | QR = paid ($5/mo) | Freemium | TikTok, Instagram |
| **I Can't Wake Up** | QR/barcode + puzzles | Included | Free + ads | Minimal |
| **Puzzle Alarm Clock** | Math/memory puzzles | No QR | Free + ads | Minimal |
| **Sleep Cycle** | Sleep tracking + smart alarm | No QR | $30/year | Instagram, X |
| **Alarm Clock Xtreme** | Math/QR/shake | QR included | Free + ads | Minimal |

**ScanAlarm's edge**: Free QR scanning with clean UX. Most competitors either paywall QR or have cluttered interfaces.

## Analysis Template

For each competitor, track:

### Social Media Audit
```
Competitor: [Name]
Platform: [TikTok / Instagram / X]
Handle: @[handle]
Followers: [count]
Posting frequency: [X/week]
Last post: [date]
Content types: [list]
Avg engagement rate: [likes+comments / followers %]
Top performing post (last 30 days):
  - URL:
  - Views:
  - Hook style:
  - Why it worked:
```

### Content Pattern Analysis
- What hooks do they use most? (text overlay style, opening line)
- What CTAs do they use? (link in bio, comment, share)
- What formats perform best? (tutorial, humor, POV)
- What's their hashtag strategy?
- Do they use AI-generated content or real footage?
- What are users saying in their comments? (pain points, requests)

### Opportunity Gaps
- Formats they're NOT using that we can own
- Complaints in their comments (features they lack)
- Regions/languages they're ignoring
- Posting time gaps

## TikTok Creative Center Research

**URL**: https://ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter

### How to Use
1. **Trending Hashtags**: Search alarm/sleep related hashtags, track volume
2. **Top Ads**: Search "alarm app" in Top Ads — see what paid content works
3. **Trending Songs**: Find sounds that align with alarm/morning content
4. **Keyword Insights**: Search "alarm", "wake up", "morning routine" for content ideas

### Weekly Research Routine (15 min)
1. Check TikTok Creative Center for new trending sounds (5 min)
2. Search "alarm app" on TikTok — sort by This Week (5 min)
3. Check competitor accounts for new posts (5 min)

## App Store Intelligence

### Track Weekly
- **App Store / Google Play rankings**: Search "alarm" — note position
- **Review mining**: Read 1-star reviews of competitors for pain points
  - Common complaints to leverage:
    - "QR scan is behind paywall"
    - "Too many ads"
    - "Confusing interface"
    - "Alarm doesn't work reliably"
- **ASO keywords**: What keywords are competitors ranking for?

Track follower growth trajectory for:

- Sleep Cycle's Instagram
- Any alarm app creator accounts that emerge
