## 30-Day Sprint

### Week 1: Find the Format (Day 1-7)
- Post 2-3x/day on TikTok
- Focus on: Raw Morning POV (#01) + Rage Bait (#05) — highest viral potential
- Test 5 different hook wordings for the same concept
- Track: **completion rate** per video (NOT likes)
- Reply to every comment within 1 hour

### Week 2: Double Down (Day 8-14)
- Identify top 2 performing formats from Week 1
- Create 5+ variations of each winner
- Start Stitch/Duet targeting viral sleep content
- Begin Day X Challenge series (#04)
- Target: 1,000 followers

### Week 3: Scale (Day 15-21)
- 3x/day TikTok minimum
- Launch #ScanAlarmChallenge
- Ride every relevant trending audio within 24 hours
- Cross-post all TikTok winners to Instagram
- Introduce Villain Character series (#10)
- Target: 5,000 followers

### Week 4: Push to 10K (Day 22-30)
- All-out: 3-4x/day TikTok
- Repost best performers with new hooks
- Batch-produce Avatar Reviews (#09) for variety
- Heavy comment engagement (reply to everything)
- Target: **10,000 followers**
