## Master List: 8 Heavy-Sleeper Hooks (ScanAlarm 適応版)

```
1) Hook: I tried 8 alarm tricks. Only one worked for heavy sleepers.
   Example: I tested 8 alarm methods for 30 days—only one survived.
   Psychology: Numbered specificity + survivor bias (curiosity gap).

2) Hook: These 3 tiny habits got me out of bed in under 60 seconds.
   Example: 3 five-second rituals that killed my snooze habit forever.
   Psychology: Small wins effect (micro-actions feel doable and compound).

3) Hook: Stop snoozing if you're ready to actually wake up at 5am.
   Example: Stop hitting snooze if you want your first $10k month.
   Psychology: Pattern interrupt + commitment cue.

4) Hook: I guarantee you'll wake up easier if you try this till the end.
   Example: Follow this 60-second wake-up protocol exactly tomorrow—watch what happens.
   Psychology: Authority pledge + specificity (credibility + clear action).

5) Hook: The easiest path to early mornings (no $300 sunrise lamp needed).
   Example: The easiest way to wake up at 5am—one $0 trick, no gear.
   Psychology: Effort reduction (lowers perceived friction).

6) Hook: If you want to wake up early, skip the new alarm app—do this instead.
   Example: If you want to stop snoozing, skip the loud alarms—make it physical.
   Psychology: Contrast framing (replace bad pattern with a better one).

7) Hook: To everyone snoozing 7 times in silence, this is your nudge.
   Example: Heavy sleepers refining their morning routine quietly: try this once.
   Psychology: Identity affirmation (speaks to a valued self-image).

8) Hook: I finally cracked sustainable mornings (and it wasn't discipline).
   Example: I built a morning system that survives the worst nights.
   Psychology: Relief from self-blame (it's not you, it's the system).
```

1. このリストを **720x1280縦** の画像/動画に組み込む
