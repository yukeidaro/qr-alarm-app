**Not "Top 5 Tips." One specific, counter-intuitive hack that makes people hit Save.**

## Why It Works
- Saves = 2x algorithm weight. Hack content has the highest save rate.
- Curiosity gap + Novelty: "wait, you can do that?"
- Positions as discovery, not advertisement. Anti-marketing.
- @thebranding.ai: "This feels illegal to know" is one of the most effective hook categories

## Production Rules
- **Screen recording is king.** Show the actual app, actual setup.
- **Talk fast.** Hack videos that drag = death. 20-30 seconds max.
- **One hack per video.** Don't stack tips. One concept = one video.
- **Voiceover OR text. Not both being verbose.** Dual-hook: bold text on screen + voiceover saying something slightly different.

## Structure (15-25 seconds)

```
[0-2s] Hook: "thing i wish i knew about waking up" or "this feels illegal to know"
[2-5s] The setup: "your phone alarm is designed to let you snooze. it's working against you."
[5-15s] The hack: "put a qr code where you WANT to start your morning. bathroom = shower. kitchen = coffee."
[15-20s] Quick demo: scan QR → alarm stops → already in the right place
[20-25s] "free app called scanalarm. save this."
```

**Psychological triggers**: Curiosity ("illegal to know") + Loss aversion ("working against you") + Novelty (QR placement strategy)
